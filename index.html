How to play

Ro.Sham.Bo is a game of Probability in which the player chooses an object to combat the computers object.The player to reach score 3 first, becomes the winner.

Rock - Rock breaks the scissor and hence wins,but it is wrapped and defeated by the paper.

Paper - Paper wraps the rock and wins, but it gets cut by scissor.

Scissors - Scissor wins against paper by cutting.

Ro.Sham.Bo by Surbhi Singh,2018.
